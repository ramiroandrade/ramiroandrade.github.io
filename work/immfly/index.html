Introduction

Clients

The company designs and builds IFE (in-flight entertainment) solutions and connected digital services. I design products for airlines such as British Airways, Iberia, Level and Vueling, but also do some designs for the company's own products.

Role

I´m the sole UX / UI Product Designer in two different teams and also collaborate in other projects with my two designer colleagues at the company.

Two different teams

In one team I'm dedicated to providing support and maintenance to existing products and in the other I spend my time designing new products.

1. New products

Shaping new products to redefine passengers' in-flight experience

Buy Onboard

This is an in-flight e-commerce where the British Airways passengers can buy food and drinks, launched on july 2021. The idea for this was to build a simple and elegant store focused on the smartphone and tablet buying experience.

Product list on a smartphone screen

When I joined the company, the base design for the smartphone screens was already approved. I designed the remaining screens and worked on defining some complicated flows, finding consistency for actions and iconography and polishing details.

Altough the focus was on smartphone devices, we also made sure there were tablet and laptop versions as good as the mobile one. For this I had to redesign some components like the filters, the sorting tooltip, the search and the menu.

A couple of the components redesigned

The product detail and checkout screens on tablet

A Pre-Order e-commerce

We are currently on the final stages of a new product dedicated to buying food and drinks before boarding. In this project I participated since the very beginning, from the ideation to the user testing. This product has some of the most complicated flows I have worked on, so it was a great challenge to take.

Because this product hasn't launch yet, I can't give any more details or show any images.

2. Existing products

Designing new features and components for existing products

In-flight Portals

The airlines I work with all have in-flight portals you can access to do things like buy WiFi packages, check info about the flight or view content like magazines or the press. We frequently update these in-flight portals, with new content, refreshed components or new features.

The British Airways in-flight portal (photo from British Airways)

New features for Buy Onboard

We are already working on new features for the Buy Onboard product: new flows for building a menu, new payment methods and the inclusion of sale promos.
