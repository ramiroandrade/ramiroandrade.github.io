UX / UI Design Front-end

No more long waits or wrong buses

Client

Nextbus is an Android app for schedules, routes and real time location of the buses of Montevideo's Metropolitan Transport System.

Challenge

Being able to show, in a simple and orderly manner, the large amount of information that a bus stop contains.

Proposal

  1. The default tab shows the next bus of each line.
  2. The other tabs show the next 10 buses of each line.
  3. Scheduled time (taken from the company schedule). When the bus doesn't have GPS enabled.
  4. Estimated time (based in real time location).
  5. By pressing on the bus you access the route and you get to see it located in the map in real time.

Roles

I was in charge of designing the logo, iconography and interface elements of the app, as well as the design and front-end development of its website (landing page).
