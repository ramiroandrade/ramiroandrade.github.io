Improving Barcelona’s
subway system UX

From the tickets to the stop indicators, a few suggestions to improve the passenger’s experience while using Barcelona’s subway system.

Client

Nextbus is an Android app for schedules, routes and real time location of the buses of Montevideo's Metropolitan Transport System.

Challenge

Being able to show, in a simple and orderly manner, the large amount of information that a bus stop contains.

2. The Forms

Building intuitive forms that facilitate completion an data edition

Roles

I was in charge of designing the logo, iconography and interface elements of the app, as well as the design and front-end development of its website (landing page).

The finding

The use of tabs was the great finding of the app's design. These facilitated the organization, navigation and “discoverability” of the many bus schedules and lines that can be found at the same stop.
