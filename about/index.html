About

A few words about my career path and some tidbits about my personality

I was born in 1989 and they called me Ramiro Andrade. I'm uruguayan but I don't drink mate, that's why I had to exile in Barcelona. I have a bachelor's degree in Advertising Communication from ORT University and a master's degree in Digital Experiences Design from BAU.

On the way to becoming an advertising creative I came across interaction design and found another way to live on creativity. Thus, for more than 9 years I've been creating all kinds of digital experiences with special focus on usability and accessibility.

In this newfound path I was also able to work as a front-end developer, learn and use various work and design methodologies, do all kinds of user tests and even corrected an error in Google's Keep app (I have proof, I choose to believe).

For more details about my training, employment and skills you can download my resume.
